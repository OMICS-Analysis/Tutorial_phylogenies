Chapter 1 Introduction

These is our main training resource for phylogeny manipulation and graphic representation. We focus on the following subjects

  • NEWICK FORMAT: Content and meaning
  • Usage of the main PACKAGES for phylogeny analysis
  • RECIPES for frequent tasks

Specific tasks

Keep in mind that phylogenies are created with two main goals:

  1. To verify the phylogenetic position of know strains
  2. To eliminate or re-label redundant or problematic strains
  3. To test different representations using the matetadata
  4. To deliver final phylogeny to customers

So it is important that phylogenetic distances, support values, as well as relevant data are clearly visible in the phylogeny images :) (Big font, color: high contrast)
